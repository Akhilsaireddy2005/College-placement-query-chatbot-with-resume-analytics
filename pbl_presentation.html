🧠 Deep Learning 💬 NLP Chatbot 📄 Resume Analytics 🌐 Flask Web App

Problem Statement

Why This Project?

😕

Information Overload

Students struggle to find placement info — eligibility, timelines, company requirements — scattered across notices & emails.

📋

Resume Blind Spots

Students lack feedback on skill gaps, domain fit, and how their resume compares to real job requirements.

⏰

No 24/7 Guidance

Placement cells are unavailable after hours. Students need instant, accurate answers during peak preparation time.

🎯

Our Solution

An intelligent chatbot + automated resume analyser that provides real-time placement guidance and skill gap analysis.
